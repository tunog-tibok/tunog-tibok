About TUNOG-TIBOK

TUNOG-TIBOK is a rising Filipino band formed on May 25, 2025, blending heart-pounding rhythms and soulful lyrics straight from the heart of the Philippines. Made up of five passionate members—Alex, Hiro, Jolo, Juno, and Kurt—the group brings together diverse musical influences and personal stories to create a sound that’s uniquely their own.

Rooted in friendship and fueled by love for music, TUNOG-TIBOK lives up to its name: the beat of the heart. Whether it’s through electrifying live performances or intimate acoustic sets, they aim to connect with listeners and echo the everyday joys, struggles, and hopes of the Filipino spirit.
